Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Dropadot is built to collect as little as possible. Here is exactly what we do and don't collect.

What we collect

  • Your approximate location, so we can place your dot on the map
  • Basic technical data your browser sends automatically (IP address, browser type), used only to keep the site running and prevent abuse
  • The text you choose to post

What we don't collect

  • Your name, email, or any account information (we don't have accounts)
  • Your precise GPS coordinates
  • Any tracking across other sites
  • Any data sold or shared with advertisers

How long we keep it

Everything you post is deleted at midnight UTC each day. Technical logs (like IP addresses) are kept for up to 30 days for security and abuse prevention, then deleted.

Who we share it with

No one, beyond the technical providers required to run the site (our hosting provider). We never sell or share data with advertisers, data brokers, or third parties.

Your rights

Because we don't have accounts, we don't have a user record to delete on request. If you believe a specific dot should be removed, contact us and we will take it down.

Cookies

Dropadot uses minimal cookies, only for the site to function. We do not use tracking cookies or analytics cookies that follow you across sites.

Children

Dropadot is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.
